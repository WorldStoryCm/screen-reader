# Design Context

**Target audience**: Casual gamers learning Japanese through gameplay. Not language students — people who want to understand what's on screen while playing.

**Use cases**: Capture game screenshots, OCR Japanese text, translate unfamiliar words, build vocabulary cards, track learning progress casually.

**Brand personality**: Warm / Crafted — like a comfortable study desk with good lighting. Not clinical or technical. The interface should feel cozy and inviting, encouraging regular use without pressure.

**Aesthetic direction**: "Warm Study Desk"
- Dark mode: warm dimly-lit study room (amber-tinted neutrals, no dead grays)
- Light mode: cream notebook under warm lamplight
- Accent: amber (study lamp glow)
- Typography: DM Sans — warm geometric, readable at small sizes
- Color system: OKLCH with warm-tinted neutrals (hue 65-75)
- All neutrals carry a subtle amber warmth — no pure black or pure white

**Constraints**: Tauri 2 desktop app (macOS/Windows), React + Tailwind v4, shadcn/ui components, must work well alongside fullscreen games.
